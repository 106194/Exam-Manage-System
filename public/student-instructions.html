Student Instructions

Back to Dashboard

Attempt Exam

On your dashboard, select an exam. If the exam is protected, enter the password provided by your teacher. You will have limited time based on the exam settings.

Result

After submitting the exam (or auto-submission upon timeout), you will see your total marks and performance. Leaderboard will reflect your rank if enabled.

Practice Exam

You can reattempt any completed exam in practice mode. Marks from practice mode will not affect leaderboard or your profile stats. Your leaderboard will be shown in disqualified block.

Update Settings

You can edit your Full Name, Institution, Bio, and Profile Picture. You can reset your password using email verification if forgotten. You can't change your username directly — contact the admin to request a username change.

Privacy & Policy: Your answers and scores are securely stored. Do not try to refresh or reload the exam page—doing so may disqualify you. You can hide your scores in every exam from your profile. Never share your email or password with others.
